what do we do?

Creating Joy is an instruction based art studio where children get to "shine" and enrich their imaginations! It is designed as an avenue for children to express themselves, develop skills, and share their artwork within the community.

meet the instructors!

Katie grew up always loving art and wanting to teach. She has been a pre-school teacher and teaching art classes since 1995. This combination has given her the ability to work with kids, especially their fine motor skills and making the complex simple. Katie and Brent met in high school, sharing many art classes together.

MRS. KATIE

Brent grew up always wanting to be a designer. He has designed logos, websites and advertisements for several small businesses including his own and Creating Joy. Brent has worked with kids as a private art teacher, substitute and coach for 30 years. Katie and Brent have two kids, Josiah, who graduated college as a sport business major.
